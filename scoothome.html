The 1986 Wilderness Beaver upon my purchase (aug 2022). Bought in Bradford-on-Avon Uk from Ian Graham, the inventor and builder of Wilderness Boats. It is a fiberglass narrowboat at 23 feet with a draft of 8 inches. The Wilderness boats were designed to be trailered, thus lightweight and flatbottomed.


As of writing this, the improvements one year into its ownership include:
-Static bed
-Wood burning stove
-Forward bulkhead
-Solar
