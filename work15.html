Repair Cart for MONC

summer 2024

Alice and I made this mean modular machine for spectacle repairs out in the field. It’s made of aluminum square section, hemp roof tiles, recycled plastic sheet, and stainless steel hardware. The polishing wheel, dremel, and frame heater are powered by a solar panel on the roof and a 1100w inverter under the hood. I’m proud of this collaboration.
